About NABTEB

The National Business and Technical Examinations Board (NABTEB) is responsible for conducting examinations in technical, vocational, and business education in Nigeria. NABTEB certificates are recognized for admission into polytechnics, colleges of education, and universities.

  4. Study Past Questions: Familiarize yourself with NABTEB question patterns
