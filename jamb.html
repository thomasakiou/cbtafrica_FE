About JAMB UTME

The Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) conducts the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME) for candidates seeking admission into Nigerian universities, polytechnics, and colleges of education. JAMB UTME is a computer-based test (CBT) that assesses candidates' readiness for tertiary education.

Preparation Guide

  1. Know Your Subjects: Choose the right subject combination for your course
  2. Practice CBT Format: Get comfortable with computer-based testing
  3. Master Use of English: This is compulsory and crucial for all candidates
  4. Time Management: Practice completing 180 questions in 2 hours
  5. Take Mock Exams: Simulate real exam conditions regularly
  6. Review Past Questions: Study JAMB past questions extensively
  7. Stay Updated: Follow JAMB announcements and syllabus changes

Scoring System

Each subject has 60 questions worth 100 marks. Your total score is the sum of all four subjects (maximum 400). Most universities require a minimum of 180-200 for admission consideration, though competitive courses may require 250+.
