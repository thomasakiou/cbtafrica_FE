About CBT Platform

Our Computer Based Testing platform is designed to help students prepare for major Nigerian examinations. The platform simulates real exam conditions and provides instant feedback to enhance learning.

How to Use the Platform

  1. Create Account: Register with your email and create a secure password
  2. Login: Access your dashboard with your credentials
  3. Select Exam Type: Choose from NECO, WAEC, JAMB, or NABTEB
  4. Configure Test: Set your preferred time duration and number of questions
  5. Take Test: Answer questions within the time limit
  6. Review Results: View your score and review correct answers
